How it works

Simplify home cooking in 4 easy steps.

1. Select your box

Whether you’re cooking for two or feeding a
family, we have flexible box sizes to fit your
every need.

2. Choose your recipes

With your box size determined, you can now
select your recipes from the 20 delicious
dishes on our menu. Take control of your meal
planning and look out for brand-new recipes
every week!

3. Receive your delivery

Choose from 5 delivery days to suit your
schedule and stand by for your Hello Chef box
to arrive. If you need to change your delivery day
or skip a week, you can do so easily in
your account settings

4. Cook, eat and enjoy!

Once your box has been delivered with your chosen
recipes and pre-packed ingredients,
all that is left to do is for you to cook! Enjoy a
fuss-free cooking experience with our simple
and delicious recipes.
